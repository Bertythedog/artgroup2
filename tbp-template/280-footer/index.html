About Us

Our Beginnings

Welcome to the Horsley & Clandon Society of Arts Website, not to be confused with Horsley Art Society (ex NADFAS), although many members belong to both. Our Society was founded in 1966 and is a friendly and enthusiastic Society of nearly 100 members, both painters and non painting art lovers, who meet on the last Wednesday of every month in the East Horsley Village Hall. We enjoy demonstrations and lectures on a variety of subjects – from watercolourists, acrylic and mixed media painters, oil painters and art historians. We run several workshops a year and hold a hugely successful exhibition in the Autumn. We also meet the first Friday of each month in the East Clandon Church Hall for ” Pub and Paint “, when we paint from our own references and visit the pub for lunch.
New members are always welcome, whatever their abilities or interests. Visitors are also very welcome and can attend for a small fee. Currently the annual membership fee is £25
