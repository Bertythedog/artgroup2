About Us

Welcome to the Horsley & Clandon Society of Arts Website, for Artists and Art Lovers. Not to be confused with Horsley Art Society (ex NADFAS), although many members belong to both. Our Society was founded in 1966 and is a friendly and enthusiastic Society of nearly 100 members, both painters and non painting art lovers, who meet on the last Wednesday of every month in the East Horsley Village Hall. “We enjoy demonstrations on a variety of subjects covering all mediums and also lectures and slide shows from art historians” .We run several workshops a year and hold a hugely successful exhibition in the Autumn. We also meet the first Friday of each month in the East Clandon Church Hall for ” Pub and Paint “, when we paint from our own references and visit the pub for lunch.
New members are always welcome, whatever their abilities or interests. Visitors are also very welcome and can attend for a small fee. Currently the annual membership fee is £25

Workshops

One day workshops are held several times a year for Artists and Art Lovers on a Saturday in the Poppy Room of the Royal British Legion Hall at West Clandon, opposite the Onslow Arms. They cover various mediums and are led by professional artists. There is a fee charged for these. The programme will be published later in the year.
